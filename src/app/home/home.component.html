What's Registro ?
Registro is a Web App where the Gitam Clubs can register students for the events and Authenticate them by scanning their QRs during the event.
Why Registro ?
    Registro makes a lot of things easier mainly because of the following features:
  • Register students using their Student Portal Credentials.
  • Scan QRs of people to check-in during event.
  • View/Search all the registered people for the event.
Instructions
  • Request for an Admin account by mailing me from your club email.
  • Use your Admin account to add Events HERE.
  • Use the Stall accounts you provided, to register students.
  • Collect payment from students.
  • Use Stall accounts to Scan QRs of Students to verify their registration on event day.
